About our School

Walpole High School, in Walpole, Massachusetts, is a public high school with strong academic programs. With students between ages 14-18, the school offers many courses and opportunities, such as AP classes, Honors classes, and many clubs. The Walpole Timberwolves have constant division 1 and 2 championships.

Our Students

Our students have constant academic success, glorified by great teachers, a good curriculum, and many other aspects to encourage these children to learn and grow their minds. Many courses tailor towards their future, such as classes like accounting and 21st century living, which teach about how life is in the real world and how to do things that most young people need help with.
